Questions For The Owner
Keegan Masser

Why was Yard Logo started?

What is your background with art?

Keegan took PSEO classes as a senior and had a lot of free time on his hands, so he decided to recycle an old idea and turn it into a buisness. YardLogo began in late spring/ early summer of 2021 and as of January of 2023 YardLogo offically became an LLC through the state of Minnesota.

Keegan experimented in painting college logos for grad parties when he painted a kansas "KU" for his sisters graduation party. Since the start of YardLogo, he served 19 parties in 2021, 36 parties in 2022, and now that he has mastered the technique, he plans to have another successful summer working for YardLogo.
